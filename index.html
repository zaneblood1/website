Welcome to the personal website of Zane Blood! I graduated in 2023 with a bachelor's degree in physics from the College of Arts and Sciences at Cornell Universiy in Ithaca, New York. As an undergraduate, I mainly worked in an experimental accelerator physics laboratory designing and machining parts for our group's table top accelerator used for ultrafast electron diffraction experiments. After graduating, I worked for 2 years as a fullstack software developer at Epic Systems in Verona, Wisconsin.

Starting in September 2025, I will begin my PhD in physics at the California Institute of Technology. I am interested in computational physics and using computers to create simulations of physical systems. I hope to pursue these interests of mine during my graduate education and onwards.

I primarily use this site as a testing grounds for basic front end web development with HTML, JavaScript, and CSS. In the future, I plan to add a SQL backend and turn this into a fullstack project. In the meantime, this webpage mainly just serves as a gallery for some of my favorite programming and physics projects I have worked on. Please feel free to contact me! Links to my GitHub repositories, YouTube channel, and LinkedIn can be found at the banner at the bottom of this site.
